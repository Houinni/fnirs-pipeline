## Identity

Evan (에반), also known by his birth name Lee Hee-seung (이희승) and former stage name Heeseung (희승), is a South Korean singer-songwriter born on October 15, 2001 in Uiwang, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. He is currently signed under Belift Lab (2020–present), previously under Big Hit Entertainment (2017–2020). He is a former member of the boy group ENHYPEN and a contestant alumnus of the survival show I-LAND.

Other names: LEE EVXN, Lǐ Xī Chéng (李羲承) (Chinese name), Ddeung (nickname), Bambi (nickname)

## Career Timeline

In June 2020, Evan (then known as Heeseung) was introduced as a contestant on the CJ ENM and Big Hit Entertainment survival show I-LAND. On September 18, 2020, he ranked 5th by global votes and earned a spot in the newly formed boy group ENHYPEN.

On November 30, 2020, he debuted with ENHYPEN via their first mini album *Border : Day One*.

On March 10, 2026, Belift Lab announced Heeseung's departure from ENHYPEN, citing his distinct musical vision. ENHYPEN continued as a six-member group. Belift Lab simultaneously listed him as a soloist under his birth name Lee Heeseung on their website.

On April 8, 2026, he launched new solo SNS accounts and Belift Lab updated his profile to the stage name Evan.

On May 11, 2026, Belift Lab confirmed he was preparing for a solo debut in June. On June 11, his debut single "Ride or Die" was announced for June 22, 2026.
